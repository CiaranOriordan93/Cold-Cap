Cold Caps

We reveal what the earth has hidden in snow

Specialized in polar travel, our agency 66 ° Nord organizes many stays in the Far North.

Our team is made up of experts from the Nordic countries who are very familiar with the destinations they offer you. Thus, you go with specialists, who do their best to offer you cruises and dream expeditions in wonderful and unspoiled lands. The stays of our agency are accessible to a larger number, hikers confirmed families with children or groups. Several offers are available on our site so that you can find the one that suits you. Find your happiness on our site!

Norway, Iceland, Greenland, Discover our destinations!

At 66 ° North, our guides and tour operators are true experts in the Far North. For each country, we have specialists within our team. For us, it's essential to provide you with quality Nordic trips and expeditions. Starting with our agency, you can explore the wilderness of Finland, hike through the highlands of Iceland or cross the Arctic Circle aboard a boat in Antarctica. We also offer sea kayaking in Greenland, trekking in Norway but also a weekend in the heart of the Swedish nature. On our website, you will find all the destinations selected by our guides.

Adapted stays to discover the great north!

Since 1998, our agency puts its expertise of the Nordic countries and its know-how at your disposal. We organize accompanied trips guided by our guides during which you participate in many activities, such as trekking, kayaking, Nordic ski tours or hikes. You also find tailor-made trips in the Far North that you can organize yourself by choosing your itinerary and your program while benefiting from the advice of our experts. And if you prefer to sail on the polar seas and oceans, we have several cruises in the Arctic and Antarctic that will allow you to live adventures out of the ordinary and admire breathtaking northern landscapes.
